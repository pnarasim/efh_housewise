Housewise Summary

As responsible citizens and homeowners,we should all be constantly cognizant(aware) of our consumption of water & electricity.

We should also be aware of the quality of air and noise pollution in our homes and take necessary action if these cross acceptable limits.

The project demonstrates implementing embedded systems to provide a household monitoring solution.

User Benefits

A system like this could provide the user with information required to make responsible decisions about their lifestyle choices.

Block Diagram of System

1.Intel Edison board

2.Electronut Bluey boards

3.REES52 Sound Detection sensor Module

Results

The results display the charts of electricity & water consumption, as well as the noise and CO2 levels in the environment.
